My Favorite Coffee Shops in Columbia, Missouri!

This is a list of the best local coffee shops in Columbia, Missouri, those frequented by students and community members alike.

1. Lakota Coffee Company

A premier coffee shop, Lakota offers several locations that serve smooth roasts with the best temperature, all beans roasted by hand in house.

Locations include the Downtown location at 24 S 9th St. Columbia, MO 65201, the South location at 3111 Green Meadows Way, Columbia, MO 65203 and the Hospital location at the coffee kiosk at the MU Hospital on 1 Hospital Drive, Columbia, MO 65201.

Contact Downtown Location at (573) 874-2852

Contact the South Location at (573) 499-9901

Conact the Hospital Location at (573) 884-4766

2. Shortwave Coffee

A coffee shop and roastery, Shortwave serves and roasts delicious coffee using the highest quality beans, all ethically sourced from around the world!

Located at 29 S 9th St. Columbia, MO 65201

Contact at (573) 214-0880

3. Acola Coffee Company

Locally owned, Acola serves authentic and ethnically sourced beverages produced by the Onyx Coffee Lab.

Located at 300 N St. Suite 100, Columbia, MO 65201

Contact at (573) 447-850

4. The Grind Coffee House

Described as the neighborhood's coffeeshop, The Grind was born out of a love for all things coffee, committed to providing high quality drinks personalized for each customer.

Locations include the South location at 4603 John Gary Dr. #1, Columbia, MO 65203, the North location at 2601 Rangeline St. #101, Columbia, MO 65201 and the Forum location at 1412 Forum Blvd. Suite 135, Columbia, MO 65203.

Contact the South location at (573) 447-3333

Contact the North location at (573) 447-6666

Contact the Forum location at (573) 777-8999

5. Fretboard Coffee

This coffee shop features award-winning coffees and specialty drinks and has a coffee truck named "Marilyn" that serves coffee, espresso drinks and cold brew at the farmer's market and other local events.

Located at 1013 E. Walnut St. #10, Columbia, MO 65201

Contact at (573) 227-2233

6. Coffee Zone

Described as a unique Columbia tradition, family-owned and operated, Coffee Zone offers various styles of coffee and a wide array of Mediterranean fare.

Located at 11 N. 9th St., Columbia, MO

Contact at (573) 449-8215

7. Love Coffee

The mission of Love Coffee is to serve great coffee while providing job skills training and employment in an atmosphere of love to individuals with disabilities and barriers to employment. Everything is handmade from scratch!

Located at the Tiger Hotel, 23 S 8th St., Columbia, MO 65201 and 15 Business Loop 70 E., Columbia, MO 65203

Conact at (573) 777-1877
